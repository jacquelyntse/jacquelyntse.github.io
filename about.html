About

Hello, I'm Jacquelyn!

I am a human-center designer based in San Diego, CA. I am currently a Lead UX Strategist at Solar Turbines. Previously I was doing front-end development for ASI -Cal Poly Pomona and NextGate Solutions.

I received a B.S. in Computer Information System from California State Polytechnic University, Pomona. I continued my education and completed two post-grad certifications in User Experience and Marketing Analytics from UC San Diego – Extension.

With a background in development and design, I am drawn to projects that require a high level of design thinking, craft, and vison. I am interested in how the impact of designs in products can make a difference in users’ every life. I aspire to be an empathetic designer who utilizes my technical and design skills to make meaningful informed changes by gathering actionable insights.

Feel free to contact me if you want to work together or learn more about my projects. You can email me at jzbtse@gmail.com, or find me on LinkedIn.

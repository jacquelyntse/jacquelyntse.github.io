About

Hello again! I'm Jacquelyn, a designer based in San Diego


I specialize in crafting user-centric designs as a UX Designer. Every pixel, every interaction, every emotion evoked by a user interface - that's where my passion lies. I believe that understanding the user is the key to designing impeccable digital experiences.

I had the privilege to nurture my technical and analytical skills while pursuing my B.S. in Computer Information System at Cal Poly Pomona. This background allows me not just to envision beautiful and intuitive interfaces but also to understand the intricacies of the technology that drives them.
